About Us

WBCAN

The West Bengal Covid Aid Network (WBCAN) is a citizen's initiative to try and put together resources for Covid patients across the whole of West Bengal. We are constantly verifying and updating leads of various healthcare services for every district. We were featured in Times Of India. If you are interested to help us, please register as a volunteer using the form.
